Where do you love to hangout?

We'd love to know about your favorite hangout spot. It can be a restaurant, park, bar, surf spot, ancient ruin, your favorite spot on the beach or anything else you think fit. Let us know why you like it so much.

Post photos of such places on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram as part of our social media contest and win up to $200 in prizes. Isn't that cool? Maybe you already used to share such pics on social media profiles, but now you can win amazing prizes for doing just that.

3. Use the hashtag #ylccontest in your post.
