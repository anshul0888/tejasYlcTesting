Article Submission Guidelines

Your Local Cousin’s blog is a collection of carefully curated articles written by travel enthusiasts around the globe. We seek to publish articles filled with unique information about a location, with a personalized approach. Our priority is to offer information from a local’s perspective aimed at travelers looking for a bespoke experience. Local insights on our blog help attract travelers planning their vacation to cities across the globe via

www.YourLocalCousin.com

Guidelines

We currently offer $60 (US) for an article with over 1,000 words with at least three (3) high-resolution images / videos that are yours or that you have permission to use. If you'd like us to add back links to your blog, website, social media profiles or mention your business then we pay you $30 for the article. Further, if your work is stellar (i.e. your work has been published in national/major publications or you have a popular blog etc.) and you have a strong social media following or you take very high quality photos that could grace the pages of National Geographic, contact us and we can work out a price - and send us a writing/photo sample!

Next steps:

Step 1: Submit an idea for your article along with any writing samples (hit “submit an idea” below). A writing sample can be an article that you wrote that was published OR even just a paragraph about why you love your city.

Step 2: After we approve it we will notify you by e-mail and you can get to work!

Step 3: Send us your article, at least three high-resolution images and hyperlinks via e-mail

Step 4: We have the right to ask you to revise the article and can reject it for any reason* (see reasons for rejection below)

Step 5: Once approved we will pay you immediately via your Paypal account or other method of payment

Step 6: We will market your post and hope you will too! Tell local businesses you write about that you love them – the more people and places you mention the better!

***Frequency: we will try to publish posts every few days

Our readers love engaging content that are easy, interesting reads. Some popular formats include:

• Top 10 lists (or top 7 lists!)

• A deep dive into a unique destinations (i.e. Tallinn Estonia) or the unique side of a very popular destination (i.e. street art in Chicago)

• Money saving tips for a particular city

• Unique activities that are popular in a certain city (i.e. cliff diving in Jamaica)

• Topic specific articles (i.e. pet travel, vegetarian friendly restaurants, travel with kids, senior citizen travel etc.)

• Articles about upcoming events, festivals or fairs (i.e. Oktoberfest, Rio’s Carnival, Mardi Gras etc.)

The article must:

• Be original and include current content. We will not accept previously published pieces.

• Include at least 3 high-resolution photos along a brief description for each one. We prefer they are your own photos. However, if the photos are not your originals then send us proof of permission (Your Local Cousin is not liable for any photos you send us that are illegal to publish and copyrighted). NOTE: Your Local Cousin reserves the right to use your original photos on its social media accounts. Of course we will give you credit by tagging or mentioning you in the posts.

• Do not submit photos with any filters, keep them natural and bright – if you don’t have any then you can ask friends, family or people online if it is ok to use theirs and give them credit (e.g. Yelp users often take good photos – if you have an account you can message them and ask for permission to use them - send us their written consent.)

• Photos should not have any watermarks or text.

• Include backlinks/hyperlinks to any businesses or places mentioned

• You may NOT link to Wikipedia, TripAdvisor, CouchSurfing, Craigslist, travel or restaurant review sites etc.

• You may link to your personal blog or business

• Video: if you have high quality video content feel free to add the link or embed it into the post

Photos

• It is best to use your own photos. Please do NOT sent photos that are blurry, out of focus or that have poor lighting

• Ask people to use their images if you don’t have any. You will be surprised what you will get if you just ask! If you find some great images online, contact the publisher and ask if you can post it and give them credit! We will only accept them if they say YES!

• There are many websites that allow you to use their high- resolution images for FREE! See the article below (some sites include Unsplash, Gratisography, Picjumbo, Snapwire Snaps etc.):

https://bootstrapbay.com/blog/free-stock-photos/

***make sure you can use the photos for commercial usage

Reasons for rejecting an article:

• We do not edit articles with many grammatical and spelling errors (one or two here and there are ok)

• If you are not a native English speaker do NOT insert your article into Google Translate and send it to us – we will reject it – instead have a friend help you translate it!

• You plagiarized (copied) someone’s writing

• Your writing is offensive (i.e. we do not allow discrimination based on nationality, religion, sexuality, gender, etc.) – keep politics out!

• Your article is poorly written! See our existing blog to get an idea of what we publish!

Articles will be moderated in accordance with these guidelines and may be checked and edited for minor spelling and grammar. Your article will be published within 3-10 days unless otherwise stated. If you have any questions at all, feel free to email Your Local Cousin editors Aarti and Kunal at info@yourlocalcousin.com
