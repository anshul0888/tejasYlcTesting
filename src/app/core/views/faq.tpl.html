Frequently Asked Questions

Travelers

General

Is there a process for verifying the 'local cousins'?

We take multiple steps to verify our 'local cousins'. Profiles once created are not published on our website directly. Instead they go through a vetting procedure which includes and is not limited to a Skype call with the Company and a social media and Linkedin profile verification. Once the local cousin's profile has been vetted only then do we make the profile public on our website. Incomplete profiles and profiles with poor English are removed from our system.

I can’t find locals in the city I’m traveling to?

Please contact us here or email us as info@yourlocalcousin.com and we will get back to you when we have a local cousin for the city you’re traveling to.

None of the locals have the same interests as me!

Please contact us here or at info@yourlocalcousin.com and we will do our best to find you someone within 48 hours.

What do I do if I need information on more than one city?

If you are traveling to more than one city you will have to place an order with a local cousin from that respective city. Once the order is placed you will be able to contact the local cousin and get the information you’re looking for.

How many questions can I ask my ‘Local Cousin’?

This depends on which pricing plan you select. If you select the ‘Text a local 20 questions’ you can ask 20 questions, duh! For other pricing plans advertised on our website, please click here for our latest prices.

If I’m not satisfied what do I do?

We take feedback from our customers very seriously. If you are not satisfied with the information you received from your local cousin, please contact us and we will look in to the matter and get back to you within 48 hours.

I’m having technical issues with www.YourLocalCousin.com

Go here and click on Forgot Password if you’ve forgotten your log in details and would like to re-set your password. If you’re facing other issues, you can contact us here or send us an email at info@yourlocalcousin.com and don’t forget to add a brief description of the problem you’re facing.

Communicating with a ‘Local Cousin’

How can I connect with my ‘Local Cousin’?

You can connect with your local cousin over a voice call, email or text. More information on how you can connect can be found here

Do I need to write down all the information my ‘Local Cousin’ gives me during a conversation with me ‘Local Cousin’?

No need! Your local cousin will send you a summary of the recommendations made after your voice call.

What happens if the conversation with my ‘Local Cousin’ goes past the time limit?

You will need to sign up again and purchase additional time in blocks of 30 minutes. Once you purchase additional time, you and your local cousin can schedule time to speak at a mutually convenient time.

My call was shorter than what I paid for! Can I call my ‘Local Cousin’ for additional questions?

Yes you may contact your local cousin for any additional questions but only up to the time remaining. When scheduling another call with your local cousin, please consider a time convenient for them.

Suppose my call was shorter than expected - can I keep the credit towards another local cousin?

Unfortunately not. Once you speak with your local cousin and they send you the summary after your call, your order will be considered complete and you will not be able to keep left over minutes for another local cousin you intend to speak with.

How quickly will my ‘Local Cousin’ get back to me?

We strive for a turnaround time of 48 hours and please take into account time zones. Sometimes your local cousin may have prior commitments just like you and I, therefore we appreciate and thank you for being patient while your local cousin responds back to you.

How do I schedule a conversation with my ‘Local Cousin’?

You and your local cousin can schedule a time and day mutually convenient to each other by messaging each other using our internal messaging system. You and your local cousin can also decide how you’d like to communicate with each other. In the past our users have connected with each other over Skype, Google Hangout, Viber, WeChat and Whatsapp call.

Suppose I have additional questions after my conversation with my ‘Local Cousin”?

You must sign in and purchase a pricing plan through our website in order to get in contact with your local cousin

My ‘Local Cousin’ missed our call! Now what?

We apologize for that. It’s rare that a local cousin does not show up. Please do not hesitate to contact your local cousin to schedule another time and day most convenient to you. If you run into further problems connecting with your local cousin, please contact us.

Communicating while on my trip

If I choose to text with a ‘Local Cousin’ while on my trip how many questions can I ask?

You can ask up to 20 questions from a Local Cousin if you select the ‘Text a Local’ option. These questions may be asked over standard text messaging service, Whatsapp, Skype chat etc.

Why would I choose to text questions to a ‘Local Cousin’ while on my trip?

When you reach your destination you may need on the spot information which you may have overlooked to ask your ‘Local Cousin’ before or you may get lost or need help in narrowing down from a list of activities that you heard about when you reached your destination. For these reasons, we give our travelers an option to connect with local cousins over text.

If I have an emergency on my trip can I contact my ‘Local Cousin’?

It depends on the nature of the emergency. If the emergency is life threatening or can put you in the harms way then you should contact the local authorities in your destination first. If your questions involve directions or advice on what to do, how to go about etc. you are more than welcome to text your ‘Local Cousin’.

Do I have to pay for roaming charges if I want to text my ‘Local Cousin’ while on my trip?

Yes, you and the ‘Local Cousin’ are responsible for call, data, roaming and other such charges levied on you by your respective phone companies.

Local Cousins

General

What should I do once I have completed my profile?

You can add a video talking about yourself if you have not done so. Adding a video to your profile gives you more credibility and puts you on the top of the page thus increasing your chances of getting selected by a traveler.
You can also promote your profile on your Facebook and Twitter handles. We have added social media links on your public profile and on your 'Dashboard' so you can share your profile easily.
Remember to download and review our guidelines and samples from your dashboard. You will see a green button labeled as 'Download'.
Update your Calendar (which is also on the dashboard) to indicate what dates you're not available to help a traveler. This way a traveler will not book you as their local cousin on dates you're not available to help them.

Do I have sit in front of the computer all day waiting for a traveler?

Absolutely not! You will get an email sent to you when a traveler places an order with you. Upon opening the email you will be asked to click on a link which will take you to our website. Once you log in you will be able to see the details of the order. Please ensure that you mark our email address: info@yourlocalcousin.com as safe and ensure it goes to your primary inbox and not the social or promotions folder. We will also send you a text message notifying the same.

Do you provide locals with any guidelines and samples of itineraries etc.?

Yes we do. Once you complete your profile and it has been verified by the Company you can log in and access your dashboard. At the bottom of the dashboard you will see a green button labeled 'Download'. Click on that and download the zip file. The zip file has our guidelines and samples which you can use as guidance when a traveler places an order.

Why do you ask for my phone number? Will you share my personal details with the traveler or a third party?

Your phone number is required because we send you a text message when a traveler places an order with you. This way you get notified sooner and you can respond right away to the traveler. We assure you that we will not share your personal information with anyone including the traveler.

As a 'Local Cousin' what am I liable for?

We operate in the peer-to-peer sharing economy space and provide a platform where travelers pay to get un-solicited and unbiased advice. Please click here to read our Terms of Use to understand your roles, responsibilities and liabilities.

Does the Company help me with filing and reporting the income I earn from helping travelers?

No, the Company does not provide such assistance. You are fully responsible for declaring and reporting your income and filing your taxes with the local, state and / or other authorities.

How do I become a ‘Local Cousin’?

To become a ‘Local Cousin’ please register and complete your profile HERE.

Why should I become a ‘Local Cousin’?

If you love to talk about your city and want travelers to have a great experience there, then we’d definitely like to have you on board. We think it would be a great opportunity for you to connect with travelers as you can advise them from the comfort of your home, work and anywhere in between.

Do I get any benefits to becoming a ‘Local Cousin’?

Your Local Cousin is building a community of travel lovers and people who enjoy sharing the things they love. We are constantly seeking to provide member benefits and will notify everyone in our community as we add features. In terms of “employee benefits”, ‘Local Cousins’ are not considered full-time employees and are not offered traditional benefits such as health insurance etc.

How many cities can I be a ‘Local Cousin’ for?

You can be a 'local cousin' for up to 3 cities. In order to become a ‘Local Cousin’ you must meet the following criteria: 1) You currently live in that city 2) You go back often to that city to visit family or you or a relative own property / business there 3) You went to university and graduated within the past year 4) you recently moved from a city and lived there for at least a year and know it really well. Additionally you must have strong English speaking/writing skills and speaking other languages is always a BIG PLUS!! Please note that you can not be a local for a country - only cities!

But I’ve been to 50 countries and I can advise on all 50 of them!!!

Unfortunately that doesn’t qualify you as a ‘Local Cousin’. We belive that having visited multiple cities as a tourist, does not give an individual the opportunity to dig deep into a city’s vibe, culture, history, people and lifestyle as would a person who lives there or lived there but visits frequently.

Why am I not getting selected by travelers to advise them?

Have you completed your profile? Did you add good quality photos of yourself and your city (cities) and describe who you are in detail? You can also add a 30 second video about yourself to bump up your ranking in the search results! If you haven’t you should do so asap in order to increase your chances of getting connected with travelers.

Are there opportunities to speak with other ‘Local Cousins’ as part of a community?

Yes, we plan to roll out city chapters aimed at connecting ‘Local Cousins’ in a city with each other. Additionally, we will also inform/invite you when there are travel events organized by other organizations (e.g. Travel Massive events) so you can meet other ‘Local Cousins’. Participating in the city chapters and third party events is not mandatory.

The traveler didn’t provide enough information! What should I do?

You can always message the traveler through our internal messaging system and ask them for additional information about their trip before making recommendations. Your advice will only be of help to a traveler when they tell you their travel style and interests, so don’t be shy in asking them for more information.

The traveler insists I meet them – what should I do!

Politely decline the offer. As a ‘Local Cousin’ on our platform, we strongly discourage meeting and sharing of person information with the traveler for obvious reasons. However, if you do so, keep in mind that when you registered as a ‘Local Cousin’ you also accepted our Terms and Conditions which state that meeting travelers in person is prohibited and if you do meet and should any harm come to either of the two parties, you and the traveler will hold Your Local Cousin harmless.

‘Local Cousin’ conversations

Do I need to pay for phone calls or roaming charges if communicating via phone?

Yes, you and the traveler are responsible for call, data, roaming and other such charges levied on you by your respective phone companies.

Can I choose how I want to communicate with travelers?

Yes of course. When creating your profile you can select how you’d like to communicate with the traveler. If you don’t use Skype, you may inform the traveler on alternative options such as Whatsapp call, Google Hangout, Viber, WeChat etc. through the internal messaging system.

Suppose the traveler keeps asking me questions past the time limit of our call?

You are responsible for monitoring the duration of the call. If 30 minutes are over you should let the traveler know that if he/she would like to speak for more time then they need to purchase another 30 minutes. We are unable to pay you for the extra time spent on advising the traveler if the traveler does not purchase the additional 30 minutes from us.

YLC Experiences

General

What are the booking steps?

Our website provides many of the details on each tour and experience. However, if you have any questions and want to check availability of the tour for your dates, please click on ‘Check availability’ on the tour’s web page and provide your details including your travel requirements such as date of tour, number of travelers, dietary restrictions etc. Our Sellers will then contact you with details on pricing, availability etc. You will then be directed to a website link where you can either choose to pay by your credit card or by PayPal.

How can I contact the Tour Guide / experience provider if I have any questions?

Before making a booking, if you have any question regarding the tour or experience listed on our website you can communicate by clicking on ‘Check Availability’ on the page. The Tour Guide / experience provider is responsible for answering all your inquiries. After you successfully make a booking, the Seller’s name, email address and phone number will be shared with you.

How do I know whether my booking is accepted by the Tour Guide / experience provider?

After clicking “Check Availability” you will be able to correspond directly with the tour/experience provider. You will be notified of your successful booking through the email address you registered with us and confirmed by the tour/experience provider. In the rare unfortunate circumstance that the tour/experience provider could not commit to your booking, he will contact and re-arrange directly with you another suitable time slot.

What is Your Local Cousin’s amendment, refund and cancellation policy?

Please refer to our Terms of Use

How do I meet the tour guide / experience provider?

Once you have successfully booked your tour or experience, you can arrange a meeting or pick-up directly with the seller depending on what is offered and included with your tour/experience. It can be at your hotel, or at the starting point of the tour or anywhere that you both agree on.

Who can list a tour or experience on Your Local Cousin?

Unlike other websites that offer generic tours, we bring you tours and experiences provided by individuals who are locals with several years of experience in their field and / or are licensed and qualified to provide the tour / experience. Before listing your tour you will be asked to provide evidence that you are either of the above. We carefully go through all your details including reviews, referrals and then only do we ask you to provide details on your tour. Don’t worry, we do not display your qualifications on our website but we do mention this crucial fact and to add more credibility to your profile. If you have any questions not covered here, you can always contact us directly and we will try our best to help you. In many cases we will set up conversations with tour / experience providers.
